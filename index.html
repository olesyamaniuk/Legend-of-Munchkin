About the Game

Legend of Munchkin is a fun and strategic adventure game where players explore dungeons, collect loot, and outwit their rivals. Step into the shoes of a mischievous munchkin as you battle monsters, level up, and gather treasures. The game blends humor, strategy, and endless customization options to create a unique and delightful experience.
Whether you’re a solo adventurer or enjoy teaming up with friends, Legend of Munchkin offers hours of entertaining gameplay filled with quirky twists and surprises.

How to Play

  • Merge Ingredients:

    Drag and merge identical food items to discover new recipes.

  • Decorate Your Kitchen:

    Use earned coins to customize and upgrade your kitchen space.

  • Complete Orders

    Fulfill customer requests to earn rewards and unlock new ingredients.

  • Use Boosters

    Activate boosters to speed up merging and clear challenging levels.

  • Explore and Expand

    Unlock new kitchen zones and culinary adventures as you progress.

FAQ

Is "Merge Food - Chef Decoration" free to play?
Yes, the game is free to play with optional in-app purchases for special items.
How do I unlock new decorations?
Complete orders, earn coins, and participate in events to unlock exclusive kitchen items.
Can I play offline?
Yes, the game offers an offline mode, so you can enjoy it anywhere, anytime.
What are boosters, and how do I use them?
Boosters help you merge faster or clear challenging puzzles. Earn them by completing tasks or purchasing them in-game.
Are there new updates?
Yes, new recipes, events, and decorations are regularly added to keep the game exciting.
